About


This website is to help patients reach Doctors at a lower expense. This is for a good social cause to help others.


    How can we help?
    Doctors can register with us and can help patients reach them via communication mediums at a smaller fee.






     How can we use this?
    You can use this by simply registering with us and choosing the doctor as per your need.
    Doctors will respond as fast as possible. This will save you money as well as time. Doctors will inform you if visiting a doctor physically is needed.
